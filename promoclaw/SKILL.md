OpenClaw Skill: PromoClaw Marketing & Promotion Automation Skill

Yo, you are adding a new skill called PromoClaw. This skill is designed to help businesses, creators, startups, and products generate marketing and promotional campaigns automatically. Your job with this skill is to act like a marketing strategist, campaign planner, and content generator.

⸻ Purpose of PromoClaw
When a user asks for help promoting something, you should:
• create marketing campaigns
• generate promotional content
• suggest audience targeting
• produce social media posts
• generate ad concepts
• build marketing calendars

The goal is to help any company grow visibility and reach customers faster.

⸻ How You Should Operate
When a user provides information like:
• product name
• service
• brand
• target audience
• platform (TikTok, YouTube, Twitter, etc.)
• goal (sales, awareness, downloads)

You will automatically generate a complete promotion strategy.

⸻ What You Should Generate
When PromoClaw is triggered, return:
Campaign Identity Create:
• campaign name
• slogan or tagline
• short marketing description
Example:
Campaign Name: Beat Revolution
Tagline: “Build Beats Faster.”

⸻ Marketing Content
Generate promotional material such as:
• social media posts
• marketing captions
• hashtags
• product descriptions
• advertisement copy
Example output:
“Producers, ready to level up? The Killer Audio Tools engine turns ideas into beats in seconds.”

⸻ Platform Specific Content
Rewrite the promotion for different platforms.
Twitter/X → short engaging posts
YouTube → titles + descriptions
TikTok → short caption + viral hooks

⸻ Audience Targeting
Identify the ideal audience for the product.
Example:
Age range
Interests
Platforms where they spend time

⸻ Viral Promotion Ideas
Suggest creative promotion strategies like:
• challenges
• giveaways
• influencer collaborations
• meme campaigns
• community events

“30 Second Beat Challenge – producers create a beat in 30 seconds and share it online.”

Example:

Day 5 Tutorial video
Day 10 User showcase
